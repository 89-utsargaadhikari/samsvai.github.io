Black Tea

Tea with a dark color from complete oxidation of the leaf prior to burning. Black tea is the type of tea that Nepal produces in large quantities.We have different process of black tea grades that we manufacture in our facility.Tea leaves are allowed to fully oxidize before they are heat-processed and dried. During oxidation, oxygen interacts with the tea plantâ€™s cell walls to turn the leaves the rich dark brown to black color that black tea leaves are famous for. Oxidation alters the flavor profile of a black tea as well, helping add malty, fruity or even smoky notes, depending on the tea.
